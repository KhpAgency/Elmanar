El Manar is a distinguished name among the agriculture sector with along standing along-standing history of excellence. Founded by Mr. Osama, a visionary who began his journey with a diverse range of products, our focus eventually sharpened to onions and potatoes. Over the years, our company has expanded its offerings to include a variety of citrus fruits, vegetables, and other high-quality produce. Even before the advent of modern transportation vessels, we were pioneers in the export of our products, ensuring that our produce reached global markets with the same commitment to quality and freshness that defines us today. At El Manar, we blend time honored practices with modern innovations to maintain our reputation for reliability and customer satisfaction.
